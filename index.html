Lawerence Summers

In late 2012, I was chosen to design and implement the personal website for Lawrence H. Summers, former U.S. Treasury Secretary and President of Harvard University. This included ongoing research, online strategy and client training.

Mr. Summers now uses the site to successfully disseminate his ideas to the media, students and the general public. The new site emphasized content by using a clear hierarchy and simple user interface. The result is highly professional and elegant.
